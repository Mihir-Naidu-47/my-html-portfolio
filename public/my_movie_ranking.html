My top 3 movies of all time .

MS Dhoni :The untold story

This is one of the best biopics directed on one of the best keeper batsmen of India, Mahendra Singh Dhoni.The actor playing the main character (Sushant Singh Rajput) is also an inspiration coming to bollywood (India's hollywood) as a complete outsider and making it big in the glamour industry!!!

Mirzapur

This is a village oriented thriller which is directed in the city of Mirzapur
itself in the state of Uttar Pradesh.It shows the high level politics,violence,masculinity of people in one big family of politician of that state.

Animal

This is one of the best movies i have ever witnessed it is on masculinity,violence,manhood,family bonding , romance and effects of wrong parenting.The main character Ranvijay Singh Balbir is played by the heartthroab of bollywood superstar Ranbir Singh.
